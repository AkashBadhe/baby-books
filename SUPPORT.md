## Common Issues
- App not updating:
  - Restart the app and device.
  - Reinstall the app if needed.
- Missing media or cards:
  - Ensure you are on the latest app version.
- No voice playback:
  - Enable voice from Settings.
  - Check device media volume and text-to-speech settings.
