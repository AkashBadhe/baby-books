## Full Description
FirstWords Cards is a gentle, kid-friendly learning app designed for babies and toddlers.

What your child can learn:
- Alphabet cards (A-Z)
- Number cards (1-10)
- Colors with clear visual circles
- Shapes and sizes with simple visuals
- Everyday categories like fruits, vegetables, birds, body parts, vehicles, and wild animals

Built for calm early learning:
- Soft, baby-safe color themes
- Smooth card transitions
- Big, easy-to-see visuals
- Simple tap, swipe, and keyboard navigation (web)
- Optional voice playback

Parent-friendly:
- No account required
- Easy settings
- Local progress and favorites

FirstWords Cards keeps learning simple, visual, and joyful for little learners.
