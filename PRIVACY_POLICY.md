# FirstWords Cards Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

## Summary
FirstWords Cards is designed for early learning. The app does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal data from children.

## Data We Collect
- No account data is collected.
- No direct personal identifiers are collected by default.
- Basic app diagnostics may be collected by platform providers (for example, crash logs) if enabled in release tooling.

## Local Device Storage
- The app stores learning preferences locally on the device, such as:
  - selected category
  - playback/voice preferences
  - favorites/progress
- This local data stays on the device unless the user backs up the device via OS/cloud backup features.

## Audio and Media
- The app may use text-to-speech services available on the device.
- Media files included in the app are used only to render educational content.

## Children’s Privacy
- The app is intended for children with parental guidance.
- The app does not include social features or user-to-user communication.
- The app does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services
- Distribution and operational data may be processed by:
  - Google Play (app distribution and store analytics)
  - Expo/EAS build and deployment services
- Please review those providers’ privacy policies for platform-level processing.

## Data Deletion
- App data can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in device settings.
